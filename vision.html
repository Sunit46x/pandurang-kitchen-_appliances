About Pandurang Kitchen Appliances

Introduction

Pandurang Kitchen Appliances is a reputable store in Pune that specializes in the sales and after-sale service of kitchen appliances products.

Establishment & Expertise

With three branches operating in Pune since 1992, we have established ourselves as a trusted provider of high-quality kitchen appliances.We possess extensive knowledge and experience in the appliances industry, allowing us to offer top-notch products and exceptional customer service.

Vision and Mission

Vision & Mission Statement

  • Our mission is to provide our customers with innovative and reliable kitchen appliances that enhance their cooking experience and simplify their daily lives.
  • Our vision is to become the leading provider of kitchen appliances in India.
  • We aim to be known for our exceptional quality, customer-centric approach, and commitment to continuous improvement.
